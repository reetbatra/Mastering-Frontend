Visit Bilaspur

Enjoy culture and nature in this brillant district in Chhattisgarh.

Top three activites to do at Bilaspur

Kanan Pindari Zoo

It's a zoo with lots of animals , birds , reptiles and fishes many other things to explore you can spend a half day tour with us and also you can visit wonderworld on the same day. Wonder world has a very good restaurant with a wonderful ambience and many adventure games.

Kutaghat Dam

This is very good place for picnic. Area was naturally beautiful. Good view and scenery surrounding this place. Small garden was also there.

Deorani Jethani Temple

A ruin of a temple. Group of large stones stacked up at the site. Not well maintained. Or, I would call this wildly maintained. You need some imagination to think of this place. As the location is off the highway, you need your car to visit here.
